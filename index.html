Project Summary

There are many modern applications in which spelling correction algorithms play a role, including web searching, word processing applications, e-mail and text messaging applications, and optical character recognition scanning, to name a few. We set out to research how correction algorithms work, and to develop a SPARK-based spelling correction solution of our own.
